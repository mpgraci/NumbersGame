The Goal:

Using the 9 given letters, spell the longest word possible

How to Play:
  • Choose either a vowel or consonant to add to the board. Do this until there are 9 letters.
  • You must have at least 3 vowels and at least 4 consonants
  • Drag the letters to the bottom row to form a word
  • Words must be found in the Oxford Dictionary of English, as well as accepted forms of them that may not be explicitly listed (plurals, inflectiosn of verbs, superlatvies of adjectives, etc.
  • No proper nouns allowed
  • No hyphenated words allowed

Scoring:
  • Only the player with the longest word scores
  • If tied, each tied player scores
  • 1 point for each letter
